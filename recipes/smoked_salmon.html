Smoked Salmon

Description

A delicious piece of smoked atlantic salmon, in a smoker with fresh apple cider wood.

Simple to make, sure to fill your stomach and satiate your appetite!

Ingredients

  • Atlantic Salmon
  • Apple Cider Wood
  • Lemon Zest
  • Lime
  • Rough Sea Salt
  • Black Pepper

Steps

  1. Filet the atlantic salmon
  2. Season heavily with salt and pepper
  3. Prepare the smoker by burning apple cider wood
  4. Gently place the seasoned salmon onto the smoker, then let it smoke for at least 4 hours
  5. Garnish with lemon zest and squeeze fresh lime juice over
