Indomie Goreng

Description

Indomie Goreng, the quintessential Indonesian classic! My mother always tells me not to eat too much of it but... sometimes it's irresistable. Certainly up there for one of the greatest instant foods of all time, and it's customisable, too!

Ingredients

  • Indomie Packets x2
  • Fried Shallots
  • Vegetable oil
  • Egg
  • Prawn Cracker

Steps

  1. Cook the Indomie Packet as instructed, then mix well.
  2. Fry an egg sunny side-up with sufficient oil under high heat, until a crispy bottom is attained.
  3. Plate up with fried shallots and prawn crackers on the side, and enjoy!
